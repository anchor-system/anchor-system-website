Key Maps

When learning piano in the anchor system I recommend starting with the C major scale which maps to all the white keys, under this mapping the keyboard will look like this:

Attempt to memorize where the numbers are on the map as quickly as possible, once you have a decent recollection of where each number is, start by playing through 2 5 9 0 - 7 11 2 5 - 0 4 7 11 with a metronome.
